The Trash Dudes

We are two brothers originally from Houston, TX named Austin & Alex Smyser and we are starting a business that will be servicing apartment communities with doorstep trash and recycling pick up, as well as being responsible for dumpster cleanliness - with all of this will be happening three times per week.

We also have a special mission of giving back to charity organizations, fostering an amazing team-oriented work environment, and helping pay for our employees’ college education.

We have been in the process of building a crowd-funding campaign which will be going live Sunday October 22nd, 2017 to fund our business dream and after spending months researching the industry and finding our competitive advantage in the Louisville, KY market we feel more than ready to grow this idea to something huge.
